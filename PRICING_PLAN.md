## 0. Positioning (read first)

**F&B Smart Ledger is an AI-powered smart ledger + profit dashboard — NOT an accounting or tax-filing system.**

We sell **clarity and time saved**, not bookkeeping compliance. The one-line pitch:

> *"Snap your supplier invoice, log today's sales, and see your real profit — without hiring an accountant."*

What it does for the owner:
- Track **daily sales** (dine-in, takeaway, GrabFood, Foodpanda, ShopeeFood)
- **Scan supplier invoices** with AI and auto-organize them
- Track **food cost %** and **supplier spending**
- Generate a **monthly P&L** (revenue − COGS − expenses = profit)
- **Export PDF / CSV** reports
- See business performance on one **dashboard**
- Cut **manual paperwork** (WhatsApp screenshots, paper invoices, Excel)

We are replacing the **shoebox of receipts + messy Excel**, not the accountant who files SST.

Close your first **5–10 clients** with a "Founding Member" deal. The goal at this stage is **proof, testimonials, and feedback — not margin.**

> - **Setup fee waived** (save RM199)
> - **Free data import** + free guided onboarding (we set it up with them)
> - **Price locked for 12 months** — no increase even after beta
> - **Priority access** to new features (AI scanning, multi-outlet, etc.)
>
> **In exchange, you ask for:**
> 1. Honest feedback every 2 weeks

**Why this works:** small F&B owners fear being "the first." A discount + "we'll set it all up for you" + price lock removes the risk. You get real-world invoices to harden the AI, social proof to sell the next 50, and case studies showing real RM saved.

**Practical script:** *"I'm onboarding 10 founding cafes in the Klang Valley. You get the full Pro plan at half price for 6 months, I set everything up for you for free, and your price never goes up for a year — I just need your honest feedback."*

## 6. Cost consideration — AI OCR risk (in plain words)

**Every AI invoice scan calls a paid AI vision model** (e.g. OpenAI GPT‑4o). Roughly:

- **~RM0.10 – RM0.40 per scan** depending on image size/quality.
- A cafe scanning **100 invoices/month** ≈ **RM10–40 in raw AI cost** — fine inside a RM99 plan.
- A heavy user scanning **1,000 invoices/month with no limit** ≈ **RM100–400** — that **wipes out the subscription margin**.

**Therefore, every plan has a monthly AI scan quota.** When the quota runs out:
1. The owner can still **enter invoices manually** (free, unlimited), **or**
2. Buy an **extra scan pack** (RM15 / 50 scans), **or**
3. The scanner falls back to **mock/demo mode** (no cost).

> 🔒 **Hard rule:** never offer "unlimited AI scans." Always quota it. The app already supports a `USE_MOCK_AI` switch and per-plan limits, so this is enforceable. Keep the live API key server-side only; bill scans, don't eat them.

**Margin sanity check:** at RM99 Pro with 100 scans, worst-case AI cost ≈ RM40 → still ~60% gross margin before hosting. Hosting (Supabase/Vercel) is a few RM per client/month. Healthy.

## 8. What NOT to promise ⚠️

Put these boundaries in the contract/quote and say them out loud during the sale:

- ❌ **Not a certified accounting system** — does not replace a licensed accountant or audited books.
- ❌ **Not tax-filing software** — does not file or submit SST/GST.
- ❌ **No LHDN e-Invoice (MyInvois) integration** — not yet. (Possible future roadmap, do not commit dates.)
- ❌ **Not a full inventory / stock system** — no stock levels, no recipe costing yet.
- ❌ **Staff / team accounts = Phase 4 (planned, not live)** — role logic exists, but creating staff logins ships later. Sell as roadmap with a price lock, never as "available now."
- ❌ **Multi-outlet consolidation = Phase 4 (planned)** — today each outlet is a separate business. Don't demo it as live.
- ❌ **POS integration & Grab/Foodpanda statement auto-import = Phase 4** — manual entry today.
- ❌ **Never promise "unlimited AI scans"** — always quota-based.

> Golden rule: **demo only what's actually built** (sales, expenses, suppliers, invoice scan, P&L, PDF/CSV, dashboard, EN/中文, demo mode). Everything else is "on the roadmap, and as a Premium/founding client you're first in line and price-locked."

## 9. Recommendation — which package to push first

### 👉 Lead with **Pro (RM99/month)**, sold via the **Founding Member offer (RM49 for 6 months)**.

**Why Pro:**
1. **It's the value story.** AI invoice scanning + food cost % + Grab/Foodpanda net profit is exactly the pain an *active* cafe/restaurant feels. That's where the "wow" is.
2. **It's the margin sweet spot.** RM99 covers AI scan costs with healthy margin; Starter is almost too cheap to be worth your sales time, Premium leans on roadmap features.
3. **Everything in Pro is actually built today** — you can demo all of it live, no promises required.
4. **Clear upsell ladder.** Land active cafes on Pro now → upsell to Premium when multi-outlet/staff ship (Phase 4) → reserve custom build for groups.

**How to use the others:**
- **Starter (RM39)** = the "no excuse" entry for tiny stalls/kopitiam and the fallback when a prospect says Pro is too much. Use it to get them in, then upsell once they're hooked on the dashboard.
- **Premium (RM239)** = your *anchor* — having it makes Pro look reasonable, and it captures growth-minded owners who want to be first on multi-outlet/staff.

**First 90 days plan:** push **Pro via the Founding Member deal** to ~10 cafes/restaurants in one area (Klang Valley), set them up for free, collect real invoices + testimonials, then raise to full price and sell from proof.
